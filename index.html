Who we are:
Munder Difflin Co. is a stationery and lifestyle brand based in South Park, Colorado founded and owned by husband and husband team, Mr.Garrison and Mr.Slave. The company officially launched via theRealMunderDifflin.com in November 1986. What began as a small business based out of Mr.Garrison's modest 5story mansion has quickly grown into an international brand over the few decades.
